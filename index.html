Alcemy

Data Science and Machine Learning for the Cement Supply Chain

How can we help?

Why Alcemy exists

There are several decisions you have to take in the last step of cement production. You are trying to get the 2 day or 28 day strength right, to the target corridor agreed with your customer, while at the same time having to deal with fluctuating clinker properties and inconsistent slag and fly ash qualities. All of these also have different availabilities and prices. Maybe you even purchase these from different manufacturers, not knowing what exactly to expect. Now you need to decide recipes and mill targets, while also trying to produce cost optimally. We know how difficult that is.

We have built algorithms and machine learning models from all available laboratory data to help you with these dicisions. We combine your strengths, that is understanding the reason behind abnormalities, with the strengths of algorithms, making predictions from big data.

Our system makes sure you won't produce over- or under-strong cement anymore, and makes sure you do it in the most cost efficient way.

How it works

1. Upload your Laboratory Management System Data exports or use our integration services.

2. Get Machine Learning powered product quality forecast for your cement mixtures.

3. Get suggestions for process optimization such as adjusting mill targets or clinker and slag ratios.

Leo Spenner

Co-Founder & CEO
Leo has been in close touch with cement all his life, spending many summer holidays working in a nearby plant run by his family. He holds a M.Sc. in industrial engineering from KIT, did research projects on mill grain size prediction & CCR, and has over 2 years working experience in the sector. He fights for a more efficient supply chain to ensure ongoing cement production in the EU despite its carbon footprint.
